NaiN

The final project in the course Image Processing and Image Analysis, during my 3rd year, was an implementation of a Panorama Sticher in MATLAB. The program takes 3 input images a of scene and stiches them together into a final panorama image. We used the SIFT algorithm for point finding and matching. No special distribution of the work was done, me and my partner worked together on every part in the project.
